ABOUT ORACH

After observing the area coverage of most Ugandan businesses. I realized that these businesses were not stretching beyond Ugandan boarders. The problem was not because they lacked the ability to expand. I discovered that the problem was probably the packaging and branding of these businesses. Orach aims at properly branding and packaging businesses so that they cover a wider area and reach their potential customer who is even beyond boarders.

VISSION

To elevate brands so that they can stretch beyond boarders.

VISSION

To boost the confidence of entrepreneurs through immortal faces to their brands.
